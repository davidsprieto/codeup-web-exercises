Chili Facts:

How did Chili Get its Name?

That it was named after Chile, South America by local people sympathetic to its cause during Chile's struggle for independence. The name Chile was somehow mispronounced and called Chili.

Where did Chili Originate?

The modern dish we know as chili, also known as chili con carne (chili with meat), does appear to have roots in the American West, particularly the State of Texas. An old legend holds that immigrants from the Canary Islands brought a recipe for chili with them when they settled San Antonio in the early 1700s.

What is the World's Hottest Chili?

Hormel has deemed their Ghost Reaper Chili the hottest chili in the world!
